# INDLink Launch Guide & Growth Strategy

## 1. Beta Launch Strategy (Weeks 1-2)
*   **The "Private Invite" Model:** Do not launch publicly. Instead, create a landing page that collects emails for a waitlist.
*   **Artificial Scarcity:** Give the first 500 users "Founding Creator" status, which permanently unlocks a specific premium theme or removes the 5% transaction fee on Digital Products.
*   **High-Touch Onboarding:** Personally email the first 50 creators and offer to set up their profiles for them. This guarantees high-quality initial profiles.

## 2. Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy (Weeks 3-8)
*   **Target Audience:** Indian creators selling PDFs, Notion templates, or Zoom consultation links.
*   **The "Powered By" Viral Loop:** Every free user will have a "Powered by INDLink" badge at the bottom of their profile. When their followers click it, they are funnelled to the signup page. This is the primary growth engine.
*   **Content Marketing:** Create blog posts and YouTube shorts comparing INDLink's Razorpay integration favorably against Linktree (which lacks native UPI support).

## 3. Pricing Strategy Recommendations
*   **Free Tier:** Unlimited links, 100MB storage. Platform takes a **5% + Razorpay fee** cut on Digital Products.
*   **Pro Tier (₹499/mo or ₹4999/yr):** Zero platform fees on products. Custom Themes. Advanced Analytics. 5GB Storage.
*   **Business Tier (₹1499/mo):** Custom Domains, Team Management, White-labeling (No INDLink branding), API Keys.

## 4. Scaling Roadmap
Once Product-Market Fit (PMF) is achieved:
1.  **Q3:** Introduce Physical Product selling (Shipping integrations like Shiprocket).
2.  **Q4:** Introduce an Affiliate Network where creators can sell *other* creators' products on their INDLink profiles for a commission.
